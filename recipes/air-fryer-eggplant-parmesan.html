Air Fryer Eggplant Parmesan

Description

This Air Fryer Eggplant Parmesan is a healthier twist on the classic dish, featuring crispy eggplant slices layered with marinara sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray or olive oil for frying

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 375째F (190째C).
  2. In a bowl, mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each eggplant slice in the breadcrumb mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Place the coated eggplant slices in the air fryer basket in a single layer. Spray lightly with cooking spray or drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Air fry for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
  6. In a baking dish, layer half of the marinara sauce, half of the fried eggplant slices, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
  7. Air fry the assembled dish at 375째F (190째C) for an additional 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your Air Fryer Eggplant Parmesan!
