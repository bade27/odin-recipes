Tang Pie

Description

This tang pie recipe is a refreshing dessert that combines the tartness of tang with a creamy filling, all encased in a flaky crust. Perfect for summer gatherings or as a unique treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup tang powder
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit for topping (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a saucepan, combine tang powder, water, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat until thickened, stirring constantly.
    3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Let it cool slightly.
    4. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
    5. Fold the whipped cream into the tang mixture until well combined.
    6. Pour the filling into the pre-made pie crust and smooth the top.
    7. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
    8. Top with fresh fruit if desired before serving.
